Our Doctors

Dr. Sheena Susan Andrews

Medical Director

MBBS, MS, DNB, FICO CONSULTANT (OPHTHALMOLOGIST& MEDICAL RETINA SPECIALIST)

Dr. Sheena Susan Andrews is a highly qualified Comprehensive Ophthalmologist and Medical Retina Specialist at Marian Eye Care.She brings over 15 years of experience to her practice, offering a wide range of services including thorough eye exams, precise vision evaluations, and the diagnosis of diverse eye diseases. Dr. Sheena is skilled in prescribing eyeglasses and contact lenses, performing surgical procedures, and managing conditions such as cataracts, glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration and Neuroophthalmology . Her expertise is further enhanced by a fellowship at the prestigious Giridhar Eye Institute, along with specialized skills in laser treatments and intravitreal injections.

Chief Cataract Surgeon

Our Chief Cataract Surgeon Dr Mini C G has performed more than 32000 cataract surgeries during her tenure over a period of 25 years as the Senior Consultant Ophthalmologist at Government District Hospital Kottayam.
