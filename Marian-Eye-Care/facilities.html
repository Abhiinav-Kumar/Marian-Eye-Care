Facilities at Marian Eye Care

3D Optical Coherence Tomography Device 3doct-1 Maestro2


The 3D Optical Coherence Tomography Device 3D OCT-1 Maestro2 by Topcon is an advanced, fully automated OCT system designed to streamline eye examinations with high-resolution imaging and intuitive operation. It combines OCT scanning with true-color fundus photography, enabling simultaneous structural and color imaging for a more comprehensive assessment of the retina and optic nerve. One of its standout features is its user-friendly, fully automated function—auto alignment, focus, and image capture are performed with a single touch, making it ideal for busy clinics and operators of all skill levels. The Maestro2 offers a widefield 12mm x 9mm 3D scan that covers both the macula and optic disc, providing detailed analysis of retinal layers including RNFL, GCL+, and GCL++. It also supports OCT Angiography (OCTA), which non-invasively visualizes retinal blood flow without the use of dyes.

The Haag-Streit Bern SL900 slit lamp


The Haag-Streit Bern SL900 slit lamp is a highly regarded ophthalmic instrument known for its exceptional optical quality, precision engineering, and long-lasting reliability. Designed with a convergent stereoscopic microscope, it offers standard magnifications of 10× and 16×, enabling detailed examination of both the anterior and posterior segments of the eye. The SL900 features a bright and uniform LED illumination system, delivering up to 600,000 Lux, with a tilting and rotating slit mechanism that allows adjustments in length and width for thorough observation. Its ergonomic design includes Haag-Streit's patented Cross Slide Mechanism, which provides smooth and effortless control in both vertical and horizontal directions using a single joystick—minimizing strain during extended use. Built with durable, high-quality materials, the SL900 is a reliable and essential tool for eye care professionals, delivering consistent performance over many years of clinical practice.

Keeler KSL-Z5-D Digital Slit Lamp


The Keeler KSL-Z5-D Digital Slit Lamp is a premium ophthalmic instrument designed to deliver exceptional optical performance and advanced digital imaging capabilities. Featuring a 5-step magnification system (6x, 10x, 16x, 25x, and 40x), it allows for detailed examination of ocular structures. The slit lamp is equipped with high-quality Keeler optics and LED illumination, providing consistent and bright lighting with a color temperature of 3800K, ensuring clarity and precision during examinations

Air conditioned room


An air-conditioned room refers to a space equipped with an air conditioning system that controls the temperature, humidity, and air quality to provide a comfortable indoor environment. These rooms are especially beneficial in hot and humid climates, as they help maintain a cool and consistent temperature, improve air circulation, and reduce the presence of allergens or dust.

Horizontal Autoclave


A horizontal autoclave is a high-capacity sterilization device commonly used in hospitals, laboratories, research centers, and pharmaceutical industries for sterilizing medical and laboratory equipment. Unlike vertical autoclaves, the horizontal design allows for easier loading and unloading of large instruments, trays, and materials, making it ideal for high-volume sterilization needs. Horizontal autoclaves operate on the principle of steam sterilization under pressure. They typically include a chamber where items are placed, and once sealed, high-pressure saturated steam is introduced at temperatures ranging from 121°C to 134°C. This effectively destroys bacteria, viruses, fungi, and spores, ensuring that the instruments are sterile and safe for use.
